Toronto

The CN Tower is an observation tower and is the tallest free standing building in the western hemisphere. The resturant at the top is a revolving resturant and it takes 72 minutes to complete one full revolution.

Know know as the Scotiabank Arena, it is home to the Toronto Raptor and Toronto Maple Leafs. It is also where we saw Drake and Future for the Summer 16 Tour.

Situated at the City Hall, here you will find the TORONTO sign. It looks as though it is on the edge of a pool, but it’s really a skating rink in the winter.

Niagara Falls is an awe-inspiring scenerey with a group of waterfalls. We did the Maid of the Mist tour which takes you out to the waterfalls by boat.
